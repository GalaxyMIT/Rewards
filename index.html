OUR GAME IN DETAILS

THE $MIT TOKEN

The $MIT token allows players to control the game through the gathering of resources, creation of armies, and the blitzing of bases. The $MIT token will also be the main token that users can buy and sell on secondary marketplace.

How to Obtain $MIT

The $MIT token allows players to control the game through the gathering of resources, creation of armies, and the blitzing of bases. The $MIT token will also be the main token that users can buy and sell on secondary marketplaces.

In Galaxy Blitz

NFTs will be integral to the gameplay experience with unique Spaceships, Heroes and Superweapons represented as such. Available to be bought and sold on our internal marketplace as well as on secondary markets, our NFTs will empower players to truly customize their strategies via curating units that both cater to their playstyle and open up new tactical possibilities.

MOBILE FIRST

Galaxy Blitz will be the first of its kind mobile-first designed blockchain game, allowing for more users to play2earn while they are on the move, or waiting on a subway to get to work. This gives our players the ultimate flexibility.

Gameplay experience

Diversification gameplay is our biggest advantage. Among all the current blockchain games, most of the blockchain games are relatively simple, such as farming, tower defense, blind box, etc.

Mission

Bring the ultimate Metaverse gaming experience to the player. While GalaxyBlitz is a massive multiplayer Play-To-Earn combat strategy blockchain game that values playing experience, together with the Play2Earn function, it combines the use of real-world, usable tokens with high-octane gameplay and extensive history and lore for a unique player experience.

Future plan

Our team is dedicated to providing players with a cutting-edge experience and they see great potential in utilizing VR to further improve immersion and the overall experience for their players in the future PC version.
